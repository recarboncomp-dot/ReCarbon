Who We Are

Circular carbon
reimagined for
construction.

Recarbon is a Cairo-based waste-to-value startup tackling two of the cement industry's largest problems at once: hazardous bypass dust and unabated CO₂ emissions.

Using low-cost carbon mineralisation technology, we transform these industrial by-products into high-performance, low-carbon Eco-Bricks — a greener, cheaper alternative to conventional construction materials.

Our vision is to become the leading provider of circular-carbon construction materials in the MENA region, enabling cement producers to achieve near-zero emissions while building a fully sustainable value chain.

The Problem & Solution

Two problems.
One elegant solution.

The cement industry generates ~7–8% of global CO₂ emissions and produces enormous volumes of hazardous bypass dust with no viable use. Recarbon solves both — simultaneously.

The Problem
Industrial waste & emissions with no affordable fix
Cement plants responsible for ~7–8% of global CO₂ — traditional capture is too expensive
Cement bypass dust (BPD) is hazardous, costly to store, and mostly landfilled
Existing carbon capture tech is energy-intensive and unviable for developing markets
Growing ESG pressure on cement producers without cost-effective compliance options
→
Recarbon's Solution
Capture CO₂. Mineralise waste. Build with it.
CO₂ captured directly from cement plant exhaust streams
Mineralised into BPD via our low-cost carbonation reactor technology
Outputs: high-strength, lightweight, carbon-storing Eco-Bricks
Permanent CO₂ storage + zero-waste processing + new revenue streams
